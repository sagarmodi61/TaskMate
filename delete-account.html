Delete Your TaskMate Account

TaskMate allows you to request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@taskmateapp.com
  2. Use the subject: Delete My TaskMate Account
  3. Include the Google email address used to sign in to TaskMate.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Google account profile stored by TaskMate
  • Personal tasks
  • Shared tasks created by you
  • Task comments
  • Family membership
  • Reminder settings
  • Notification preferences

Data That May Be Retained

  • Information required to comply with applicable laws.
  • Anonymous analytics (if enabled).

Retention Period

Your account and associated personal data will normally be permanently deleted within 30 days after we receive your request.
